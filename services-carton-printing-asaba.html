Carton Printing

Printed cartons made for your product

Mimshak Pak Investments Ltd. provides carton printing in Asaba for businesses that need branded cartons for product packaging, storage, retail display, shipping and industrial use. Carton printing is the process of producing cartons with your design, brand name, product details and artwork printed directly onto durable board.

Good printed cartons do two jobs at once: they protect your product through handling and transport, and they present your brand to the customer. We help Nigerian businesses get both — clean printing, solid construction and consistent finishing on every order.

Why businesses need printed cartons

  • Protect products during storage, handling and shipping
  • Present your brand professionally at the point of sale
  • Carry important product, batch and regulatory information
  • Make bulk distribution and stacking easier
