Who We Are

A trusted carton & nylon packaging manufacturer in Nigeria

Mimshak Pak Investments Ltd. is a fully registered Nigerian-based manufacturing company located in Asaba, Delta State. The company specialises in carton printing and carton packaging production for businesses that need reliable, high-quality packaging for consumer products, domestic use and industrial purposes.

Over the years, we have grown by building strong relationships with satisfied customers — through excellent customer service, reliable production and consistent quality. When a business partners with us, they get a packaging supplier that takes their product, their brand and their deadlines seriously.

Through our subsidiary, Mimshak Integrated Packages, we also focus on nylon print packaging production — helping businesses create branded nylon packaging for retail, food, household, industrial and product packaging needs.
