How Mimshak Integrated Packages Supports Nylon Print Packaging

By Mimshak Pak Investments Ltd. • 5 min read

Many Nigerian businesses know Mimshak Pak Investments Ltd. for carton printing — but our packaging story does not end with cartons. Through our subsidiary, Mimshak Integrated Packages, we produce nylon print packaging: the branded nylon bags and flexible films that food, retail and product brands rely on every day. Here is how that side of the business works and what it can do for you.

A dedicated nylon print division

Mimshak Integrated Packages focuses specifically on nylon print packaging — printing your brand and design onto durable nylon and forming it into finished bags and packaging. Having a dedicated division means nylon work gets the right equipment and attention, rather than being an afterthought to carton production.

What we produce

From bread bags and food wraps to branded retail nylon and product sachets, the division handles a wide range of flexible packaging. If your product needs a lightweight, low-cost, colourful wrap that still carries your brand clearly, this is where it is made.

In-house printing and bag-making

Because printing, forming and sealing happen under one roof, we can manage quality across the whole process and produce orders of different sizes. That control is what keeps colour, print and seal consistent from the first bag to the last.

Nylon packaging looks simple — but getting the print sharp, the seal strong and every unit consistent is exactly what a dedicated division is built to do.

One company, two packaging formats

The real advantage for customers is choice. Because Mimshak Pak produces cartons and Mimshak Integrated Packages produces nylon, you can get both from one trusted source. Many brands use printed nylon for the primary pack and cartons for cases and display — and we can advise on the right mix, as we explain in our guide to nylon versus carton packaging.

Who it serves

  • Bakeries and food producers needing branded bags
  • FMCG and retail brands wanting low-cost, high-volume wrap
  • Product brands that need flexible, printed packaging
  • Businesses that want carton and nylon from one supplier

Whether you need printed nylon bags, branded flexible packaging, or a combination of nylon and cartons, Mimshak Integrated Packages and Mimshak Pak Investments Ltd. can produce it for you in Asaba, Delta State, and supply businesses across Nigeria.
